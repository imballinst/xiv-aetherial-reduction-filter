“

Break down an item to its aetherial components.


— In-game description

Aetherial Reduction is an action unlocked by questing at level 56. It is available for any class or job. Aetherial Reduction allows the player to break down specific Collectables into Crystals, Clusters and rare materials called aethersand. The higher the collectability, the higher chance for it to yield aethersand. The specific kind of aethersand produced is fixed for a given collectable. As of Patch 5.4, hitting the max collectability of 1000 guarantees some amount of aethersand, with a potential for a bonus quantity. There are types of aethersand exclusive to Miner, Botanist, and Fisher, the first examples being   Landborne,   Leafborne, and   Seaborne. Aethersand is only used in endgame crafts from the Master Recipe Tomes. Additionally, Endwalker has added Prime Collectables that yield materials exclusively used in recipes from Island Sanctuary.

Beginning with Stormblood items, the different kinds of aethersand can also be purchased from the Scrip Exchange with Gatherers' Scrip.

Unlock

Complete the level 56 quest No Longer a Collectable by speaking to Lydirlona in Mor Dhona (X:22.3, Y:6.8). Player must be a level 56 or higher Disciple of Land.
